🎞️ We Color Emotions.

We create high-quality LUTs, presets, and creative tools specially designed for Indian wedding and prewedding filmmakers. Our goal is simple — to make your videos look cinematic, emotional, and professional with just one click.

Every product is handcrafted after grading hundreds of wedding films shot across India — from warm haldi ceremonies to moody prewedding sunsets. Whether you're a beginner editor or a professional cinematographer, our tools will help you bring that filmy color tone every client dreams of.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do these LUTs work with all cameras?
✅ Yes. Tested on Canon, Sony, Nikon, and DJI cameras — perfect for Indian wedding lighting.
Which software can I use them in?
DaVinci Resolve, Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, After Effects, Lightroom & Photoshop.
Will these work on log footage?
Yes. We include both Log LUTs (S-Log, C-Log) and Rec.709 versions.
Can I use these for commercial work?
Absolutely ✅ All products include a commercial license for paid client projects.
Do you offer refunds?
Since these are digital downloads, refunds are not possible. But support is available for file issues.
I'm new to color grading — will I be able to use them?
Yes! Each pack includes a Quick Start Guide and tutorials by Vaibhav.
